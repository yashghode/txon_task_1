Our Recommendations

  • For Office Workers: Bose QuietComfort 35 II

    These headphones are comfortable to wear for long periods of time and offer excellent noise-cancellation, making them perfect for working in a noisy environment. They also have a long battery life and can be used with a wire if the battery runs out.

  • For Musicians: Audio-Technica ATH-M50x

    These headphones have a flat and accurate frequency response, making them ideal for mixing and mastering music. They also have a detachable cable and swiveling earcups, allowing for easy one-ear monitoring.

  • For Gamers: HyperX Cloud II

    These headphones have a comfortable and durable design, and offer excellent sound quality with their 53mm drivers. They also have a detachable microphone and come with a carrying case.
